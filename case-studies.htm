Case Studies

ILS Financial — Financial Planning for Military Officers, Airline Pilots, and Veteran Professionals

These scenarios are built around the decisions that actually matter — not product discussions, but structural choices with long-term consequences.

Each collection covers a specific client profile, the decisions they face, and how the pieces interact. The goal is not to prescribe outcomes. It is to demonstrate how experienced planning approaches high-stakes, irreversible choices.

Military Officer Retirement

  • White Paper · 3 Scenarios Military Officer Retirement Case Studies Three officer retirement scenarios — SBP elections, TSP rollover decisions, survivor income tradeoffs, and the structural choices that determine long-term outcomes for senior military officers.
  • Case Studies · 3 Scenarios Reserve Officer Retirement Case Studies RCSBP election under the 90-day deadline, gray area pension optimization with NDAA 2008 early draw, and dual-career TSP/401(k) coordination with a missed USERRA claim.

Career and Income Risk

  • Case Studies · 3 Scenarios Career Fragility Planning: Veteran Airline Pilots Three cases showing how to contain structural career fragility risk — medical disqualification, furlough, and mandatory retirement at 65 — for veteran airline pilots.
  • Planning Framework 100% P&T Veterans: Income Floors & Survivor Risk CRDP/CRSC elections, SBP-DIC offset rules, and survivor income engineering for permanently and totally disabled veterans — when a 100% P&T rating creates both a foundation and a structural gap.

Business and Transition

  • Case Studies · 4 Profiles Veteran Business Owner Exit Risk Four veteran business owner profiles showing how military benefits stabilize households but don't protect against partnership gaps, minority illiquidity, or leveraged exit risk.
