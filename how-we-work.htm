The ILS Onboarding Process

A Defined Process.
No Guesswork.

This is an engineered sequence, not an improvised one. Each phase has a defined deliverable. You always know where you are and what comes next.

Matt Samson, MBA, CPWA®  |  Marine Corps Veteran  |  ILS Financial

What the CPWA® designation covers →

01

Fit Meeting

Mutual confirmation this is the right relationship. No commitment required.

02

Document Preparation

Secure document vault opened. Checklist sent. Data gathered before the first planning meeting.

03

Strategy Development Meeting

Personal Financial Overview priorities set. Statement of Financial Purpose drafted. Service tier confirmed.

04

Account Setup and Transfers

Accounts opened. Agreements signed. Transfers initiated. Client updated at every step.

05

Implementation Meeting

Complete financial plan presented. Initial Personal Financial Organizer (PFO) finalized and delivered. All strategies reviewed and confirmed.

06

Ongoing Service

Review cadence begins based on service tier. You know your next meeting date before you leave the implementation meeting.
