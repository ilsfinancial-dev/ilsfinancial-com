We Are ILS Financial

Clarity for High-Stakes Financial Transitions

At ILS Financial, we work with retiring military veterans, airline pilots, and veteran business owners navigating complex, high-stakes financial transitions.

Our approach focuses on decision sequencing — ensuring critical choices around retirement, taxes, investments, and risk are made in the right order before they become difficult or impossible to undo. Rather than leading with products or performance, we prioritize clarity, coordination, and long-term confidence in direction.

We understand the unique complexity these transitions create and help clients move forward with discipline, intention, and alignment.

MBA CPWA® Marine Aviator

Matt Samson, MBA, CPWA®

Founder, ILS Financial

High-stakes financial transitions often feel manageable—until multiple decisions begin to converge at once.

I founded ILS Financial to help retiring military veterans, airline pilots, and veteran business owners navigate these moments with clarity and discipline. My work focuses on decision sequencing—ensuring critical choices around retirement, taxes, investments, and risk are made in the right order before they become difficult or impossible to reverse.

Before entering financial services, I spent over ten years on active duty as a Marine aviator — flying the F/A-18 Hornet before transitioning to UAS and Space Operations. That background in disciplined decision-making, risk management, and preparation under pressure continues to shape how I approach financial planning today.

I transitioned into financial planning in 2015 and founded ILS Financial LLC in 2020. I continue to serve in the Marine Corps Reserves, bringing over twenty-three years of combined active and reserve experience to the work.

As a fiduciary and CERTIFIED PRIVATE WEALTH ADVISOR® professional, I help clients coordinate complex financial decisions across multiple domains — not by leading with products or promises, but by creating clarity, alignment, and confidence in direction during pivotal transitions.

How ILS Supports Business Owners in Transition

Business ownership creates opportunity — and complexity. Liquidity events, reinvestment decisions, tax exposure, and personal financial planning often converge at the same time.

At ILS Financial, we help veteran business owners navigate these moments by focusing on decision sequencing and coordination across personal and business finances. Our role is to ensure critical choices are made intentionally and in the right order before implementation begins.

This approach helps business owners move forward with confidence, clarity, and alignment — particularly when decisions carry long-term consequences.
